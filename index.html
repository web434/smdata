Services

DATA MANAGEMENT & PREPARATION SERVICES
  • Geospatial Data Management: Structuring, organizing, and maintaining spatial data types in geodatabases.
  • Data Engineering & Cleaning: Preparing raw data for analysis, cleaning, repairing invalid geometries.
  • Data Conversion & Integration: Converting data between formats and integrating from multiple sources.
ADVANCED MAPPING & VISUALIZATION SERVICES
  • 2D & 3D Cartography: Creating professional maps and 3D scenes.
  • Web GIS Application Building: Creating web maps, dashboards, and apps.
SPATIAL ANALYSIS & MODELING SERVICES
  • Digital Twin: Mapping waterways, drainage, farms, convergence point, and facilities as features, analyzing their spatial relationships for planning and modeling.
  • Walking Network Operation: Developing a topologically connected system of pedestrian walkways and nodes for spatial analysis.
  • Terrain & Surface Analysis: Creating slope, aspect, hillshade, contour maps, visibility analysis.
  • Raster-to-Vector Conversion: Generating vector line work based on classified satellite pixels.
